Archethic Research introduction

Archethic is the next generation of Blockchain which provides a truly decentralized limitless network, using TransactionChain and a new consensus named ARCH (Atomic Rotating Commitment Heuristic), to be the most secure and scalable decentralized network.

Archethic's Research Environment as described one the following pages, is designed to capitalized the research process within the Archethic Public Blockchain project. This document aims to provide information and resources, related articles and experimentations about research projects.

  • Biometrics
  • P2P/Network mecanisms
  • Collaborative work and processes
